General Terms of Application

To apply for the club , there are no specific conditions that should be present in the member that excced average students requiremenets . Most of the requiremenet are directly related to the the club's objective and will , in one way or another , be reflected directly on the club by the impact that a member could give. For this , we require some strict conditions that should apply to members in order to get accepted. Those are some of the most important points

  • Knowledge
  • Discipline
  • Creativity

Knowledge

Joining on this club requires a member's knowledge of basic programming skills along with an average problem solving mindset . Langages and frameworks don't really make a difference as in this club , we will be flexible with countless tech stacks and langages that highly matches the problem's requirment . You can prove your skills by passing a technical test (problem solving test) after your application , which will decide whether you join in or not.

Discipline

Discipline is one of the major points that a member should be aware of . As we are working on solving real world problems , such a task should come with a lot of discipline and dedication. The club's work flow is based on continuous development and integration . This requires active members that will not just work on solving those problems once and for all. A solution is basically a temporare one until we came up with the perfect one . For this members should be open to changes and to re-work on ideas several times . Which brings us to the next point : Creativity

Creativity

Creativity is one of the major keys to make our work significant and meaningfull . In a world full of developpers , coming up with a solution to problems is a daily task realeased by endless number of developpers . What really makes a solution usefull and reliable , is the way it was fitted and the creative way of solving the problem . At the end of it all , our mission is not to write code only , but to write it in an optimized way and taking the solution from different visions thanks to the creative way of thinking . Creativity is a gift , you could basically "practice" it in this club .
